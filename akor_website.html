About Akor

AUTHOR · STORYTELLER

Akor is the pen name of a student and storyteller. Her love for stories began in ninth grade. Before becoming an author, she worked as an aesthetic editor for books she admired.

Today she combines creativity, emotion, and storytelling to build fictional worlds of her own.
